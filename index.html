Complex Network Study&Presentations

Posted on 2020-03-10 Edited on 2020-08-06 In Learning

Presentation 1

Preliminary data collection presentation

Qs:

  1. What are your nodes and links?
  2. How will you collect data?
  3. Size(Nodes,Links)
  4. What Qs you plan to ask?
  5. Why do we care about your network study?

Suggestions:

  1. For each slide, insert the slide number!
  2. Cite the data source/charts of your network! Otherwise, it is plagiarism !
  3. Clarify the direction/weight of your network
  4. What is the physical meaning of typical metrics in your network?
  5. Proofread of your slides to correct typos!
  6. Each of your team members should contribute to the work!
  7. Try to practice your presentation in 5 min!
